About Us

Year of establishment- 2009

With the most comprehensive services portfolio in the industry. Bhumi Real Estate in keeping with their intrinsic relationship-centric business model, covers the spectrum of real estate. Bhumi Real Estate firmly believes that this success story owes a lot to one simple consumer insight- the customer seeks a one-stop shop that provides complete property solutions. And that is exactly what Bhumi Real Estate offers. With a single-minded focus on providing complete property solutions and nurturing long-lasting relationships of trust with clients, Bhumi Real Estate offers a variety of services that span the spectrum of property needs. This relationship-centric business model translates not merely to transactions, but also to strategic advice, appraisal, valuation, research, management in related fields and much more. Knowledge is power. That's a maxim that Bhumi Real Estate lives by. This belief has led to the company's in-depth knowledge of the industry. Our experience has resulted in a huge database through a comprehensive coverage of the region spanning diverse pin codes, streets, bylanes to mega residential complexes, offices and showrooms. Direct interaction with buyers and sellers across economic, social and demographic strata has resulted in extensive data on demand and supply in the real estate market.
